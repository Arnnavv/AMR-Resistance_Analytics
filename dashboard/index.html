FINDING 03

Clinical Setting Priority

ICU settings show the highest resistance rates, confirming that critically ill patients bear the heaviest AMR burden. Blood cultures with Meropenem resistance indicate systemic sepsis — the most severe and commercially significant use case for last-resort antibiotics.

FINDING 04

Resistance Trend Direction

Temporal analysis (2008–2024) reveals the long-term trajectory of Meropenem resistance. A sustained upward trend validates the urgency of Venus Remedies' stewardship investment and underpins the commercial case for responsible Meropenem positioning.

FINDING 05

Vulnerable Populations

Nursing home patients and older age groups show elevated resistance rates due to prior antibiotic exposure and healthcare contact frequency. Long-term care facilities represent a key channel for Venus Remedies' stewardship outreach and formulary access programs.

FINDING 06

Group Phase — Predictive Extension

The patterns identified in this descriptive and diagnostic analysis provide the feature set for predictive ML models (Classification, Clustering, Regression) in the group phase — enabling StrataMed Analytics to forecast resistance risk at the patient level and guide proactive clinical intervention.
